Overview

You recently installed a new renewable heating system and used the Boiler Upgrade Scheme (BUS). As part of our audit checks, we need you to complete this questionnaire.

We'll ask you to confirm details and upload documents about:

  • your property
  • the installation

After you submit the questionnaire, we'll review it and let you know if we have any questions.

Sometimes we need to arrange a visit to look at the heating system.

Impact on your BUS grant

Your grant could be affected under the BUS regulations if either:

  • you do not complete the questionnaire
  • you give us false information
  • testing testing testing
