What you'll need

We'll ask you for details about your property and the installation. This includes information and documents about:

  • your ownership of the property
  • the type of property, for example if it's a new build
  • the type of renewable heating system, for example if it's a heat pump
  • the process of buying and installing the heating system
  • your property's Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)

You'll be asked to upload some documents that you provided with the BUS application. We use them as part of our separate audit checks.

Documents you'll need

You'll be asked to upload:

  • valid ID
  • proof that you own the property where the heating system is installed
  • the MCS Installation Certificate
  • the Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)
  • evidence of installation costs
  • evidence of payment for installation
  • evidence of property's heat loss calculations performed by your installer
  • evidence you have received or expect to receive the value of the BUS voucher

For an existing build home, you'll also be asked to upload:

  • evidence that the property was occupied prior to installation
  • evidence of the previous heating system

For a new build home, you'll also be asked to upload:

  • evidence that planning permission has been granted
  • documentation showing the previous owner of the land and the transfer of ownership to yourself
  • documentation showing who designed and built the property
  • evidence that you have funded the construction of the property
